简介[完整]

华南理工大学工学学士（2015）、硕士（2018），英国华威大学（University of Warwick）博士（2022，国家留学基金委公派博士留学生），曾就职鹏城实验室新型网络研究部产业互联网所（2022-2023），现为暨南大学信息科学技术学院计算机科学系副教授，博士生导师。主要研究兴趣包括分布式智能系统、协同智能与可持续计算，在相关学术领域发表期刊和会议论文30余篇，获IEEE Computer Society 2021年最佳论文奖第二名（best paper award runner-up），2020年广东省科技进步二等奖；参编《Data Center Handbook: Plan, Design, Build, and Operations of a Smart Data Center》（2nd Edition）等英文专著2本，担任《计算机科学》期刊“联邦学习技术及前沿应用”专栏特邀编审，IEEE TPDS、TC、TKDE、TSUSC等高影响力期刊审稿人，NeurIPS、ICML、Bench、ISPA等国内外会议的程序委员会或组织委员会成员，TBench副主编。被列入斯坦福大学2023、2024年度分布式计算领域复合引用指标全球前2%科学家（top 2% scientist, single year）。

Biography (full)

Wentai Wu received his Bachelor and Master degrees from South China University of Technology in 2015 and 2018, respectively. Sponsored by CSC, he received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science in 2022 from the University of Warwick, United Kingdom. Since 2024 he has been with the Department of Computer Science, College of Information Science and Technology, Jinan University, as an associate professor. His research interests mainly include distributed systems, collaborative machine learning and sustainable artificial intelligence. He has published over 30 research papers and contributed as chapter author to two books in relevant subjects. He is an associate editor of TBench and also serves as reviewer for high-impact journals including IEEE TPDS, TC, TKDE and TSUSC, and as organizing committee member or PC member for conferences such as Bench, ISPA, ICML and NeurIPS. He was listed among the top 2% scientists in Distributed Computing subfield per composite citation indicator released by Stanford University (2023 and 2024, single year).
