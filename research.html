Research


I have particular interest in Distributed Systems, Distributed Machine Learning, and Sustainable Computing. I am also involved in research on general machine learning techniques such as Federated Learning, Ensemble Learning, Transfer Learning and their applications in various forms of Distributed Systems.

AREAS OF INTEREST

  • Sustainable Distributed Systems
  • Collaborative Machine Learning
  • Big Data & AI Applications
